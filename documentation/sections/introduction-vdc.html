Introduction back to ToC

VDC is a driving context ontology. It is meant to be used as a domain ontology for SOSA or the Web of Things. Signals are SOSA Observable or Actuatable properties, while static attributes are properties of Vehicle branches. The units used are declared in QUDT unit types, and do not restrict to a specific unit (e.g. qudt:LinearVelocityUnit enables the usage of km/h or mph). Likewise, Attributes that refer to a phenonmenon requiring a unit are restricted to custum datatypes refering to the correct uni system (e.g. vsso:maxPower is in custom-datatype:power)
